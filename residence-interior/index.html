Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here's what our clients ask most often.

How long does a typical project take?

Our 45-day delivery promise covers everything from design approval to final handover. Custom projects may vary based on scope, but we prioritize speed without compromising quality.

What does the 10-year warranty cover?

The warranty includes manufacturing defects on modular furniture, hardware failures, and laminate fading. It does not cover misuse or normal wear and tear. Full terms are in our contract.

Do you handle civil or electrical work?

We specialize in interiors only—modular kitchens, wardrobes, false ceilings, and painting. For civil, electrical, or plumbing, we can recommend trusted partners, but it's not included in our packages.

Can I customize the packages?

Absolutely. Our packages are starting points. We'll tailor designs to your space, budget, and style preferences during the free consultation. No upselling—just what you need.

What cities do you serve?

We deliver end-to-end in Bengaluru, Hyderabad, and Chennai. For NRIs or other locations, we offer remote design and coordination with local installers.
