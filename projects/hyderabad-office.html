Hyderabad Corporate Office

Introduced high-efficiency VRF, low-glare lighting, acoustic baffles and modular meeting rooms with occupancy sensors.

Project Facts

  • Location: Hyderabad, India
  • Size: 28,000 sq ft
  • Timeline: 16 weeks

Challenges

Legacy HVAC and low ceiling heights limited comfort and planning efficiency.

Solutions

Introduced high-efficiency VRF, low-glare lighting, acoustic baffles and modular meeting rooms with occupancy sensors.

Sustainability

LEED-aligned materials, energy monitoring and improved ventilation rates.
